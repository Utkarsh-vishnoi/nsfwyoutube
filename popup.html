NSFW YouTube

Bypass for age restricted content

NSFW YouTube is an addon that can unlock age restricted content on YouTube without creating an account.

Features

  • Replace YouTube default player with YouTube embed player
    (if you have different idea, how to do that, tell me about it)
  • Add NSFW tag after video title
  • And thats it
